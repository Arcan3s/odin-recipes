Authentic Home Made Poutine

What is Poutine?

Poutine is a Canadian food made with a combination of crispy fries, hot gravy, and cheese curds.

It is a beloved dish invented by the Quebecois in the 1950's and is most commonly enjoyed by Canadians.

What Will I Need to Make my Poutine?

Below is a list of ingredients needed to make a delicious home made poutine:

  • Vegetable Oil: A quart of vegetable oil for frying.
  • Gravy: A can of Beef Gravy.
  • Cheese Curds: The key ingredient to authentic poutine.
  • Fries: The base of poutine. Use Russet potatoes for extra crispiness.

Seven Steps to Make Your Authentic Home Made Poutine:

  1. Get a pot for your oil and place it on the stovetop.
  2. Add your oil to the pot and heat to 365*F.
  3. Warm the gravy on the stovetop or in your microwave.
  4. Once your oil is heated, add your fries to the pot and let them fry until crispy.
  5. Drain the fries onto paper towels.
  6. Place your fries on a platter and top with warm gravy and cheese curds.
  7. Voila! You now have an authentic home made poutine made right in your own kitchen! Now it's time to serve and feast away.
