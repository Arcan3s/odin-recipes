Cheesy Meat-Stuffed Lasagna

What is Lasagna?

Lasagna is an Italian dish which has been around since the middle ages.

It is a pasta which consists of sauce, cheese, vegetables, and most commonly, ground beef.

Stuff I Need to Make this Delicious Lasagna:

All the ingredients to bake the dish.

  • Meat: This meat-stuffed lasagna recipe consists of ground beef and Italian sausage.
  • Lasagna Noodles: Store-bought Lasagna noodles.
  • Cheese: Ricotta, Parmesan, and Mozzarella make this lasagna extra cheesy.
  • Tomato Products:

    A can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato paste,

    and two cans of tomato sauce.

  • Spices and Seasoning:

    Fresh parsley, salt, Italian seasoning, dried basil leaves,

    fennel seeds, and black pepper.

  • Sugar: Two tbsp of white sugar to enhance the flavor of the lasagna sauce.
  • Egg: One egg to help bind the Ricotta cheese.

Steps to Make the Super Cheesy Meat-Stuffed Lasagna:

Below is a list of steps so you can make this savoury lasagna right now!

  1. Make the meat sauce.
    1. Brown your meat with fennel seeds and black pepper.
    2. Add the rest of your ingredients and let to simmer for one hour with the lid on.
    3. Remove the lid and cook until sauce thickens.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the Ricotta cheese mixture.

    1. Combine your Ricotta cheese with shredded Mozzarella and Parmesan,

      Itatlian seasoning, and salt.

    2. Mix in a bowl and sir.
  4. Layer the Lasagna in this order:
    1. Meat Sauce.
    2. Noodles.
    3. Ricotta cheese mixture.
    4. Meat Sauce.
    5. Repeat layers until satisfied and top with parmesan cheese.
  5. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes at 375*F.
  6. Remove the foil and continue to bake for 25 minutes.
  7. Top with fresh parsley and dried basil leaves.
  8. Let the lasagna rest for at least 15 minutes.
  9. You have successfully baked your Cheesy Meat-Stuffed Lasagna!
